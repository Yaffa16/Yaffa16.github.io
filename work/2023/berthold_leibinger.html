Berthold Leibinger Innovationspreis and Zukunftspreis

TRUMPF Group, ZKM | Hertz Lab, Interactive Installation — August 2023

“Berthold Leibinger Innovationspreis and Zukunftspreis” is a collaborative interactive installation realized by Yasha Jain, Bernd Lintermann & Paul Bethge. Created for the Leibinger Innovation Prize Ceremony, this project transforms the event invitation into a living generative artwork. Each invitation card becomes part of a collective visual experience projected in real time during the award ceremony. ([weirdwithcode.com](https://www.weirdwithcode.com/work/doodlydo-k8afp-h2wg3-pys8p))

Artistic Concept

The motifs on the invitation cards were generated using AI (Stable Diffusion). Latent space interpolation crafted a visual dialogue between cards, establishing continuity across images. This reflects a poetic interpretation of quantum duality — blending wave and particle states, mirrored in the flow between artworks. ([weirdwithcode.com](https://www.weirdwithcode.com/work/doodlydo-k8afp-h2wg3-pys8p))

Technical Write‑Up

The interactive visualization and real‑time system were developed using TouchDesigner. The barcode scanning subsystem (Python) allows each card’s unique identifier to trigger a new visual stage. ([weirdwithcode.com](https://www.weirdwithcode.com/work/doodlydo-k8afp-h2wg3-pys8p))
