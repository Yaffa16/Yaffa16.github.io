Yasha Jain

New Media Artist · Creative Technologist · weirdwithcode

Yasha Jain is a new media artist and creative technologist working under the moniker weirdwithcode. She creates interactive installations, generative art, and experiments with live coding. Her work draws from programming, artificial intelligence, and human–computer interaction to explore how computational systems shape perception, control, and meaning in digital culture.

Yasha has exhibited in galleries, festivals, and experimental spaces around the world, using technology not just to automate, but to spark curiosity, reflection, and engagement. She continues to explore the creative possibilities of code as both a material and a method. In her free time she likes to play the Sitar or go for a hike.

Yasha currently lives in Karlsruhe, Germany and works as an artist & researcher for the ZKM|Hertzlab.
