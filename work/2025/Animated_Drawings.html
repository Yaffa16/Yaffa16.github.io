This project questions the authority of interpretation in art. Visitors interact with AI-driven installations that reinterpret artworks in surprising ways.

Concept

A series of artworks are displayed, each accompanied by an AI interface that produces unique interpretations for each visitor.

  • Visitors answer interactive prompts that guide the AI's interpretation.
  • The AI analyzes artworks and outputs descriptive or thematic text.
  • Each visitor experiences a personalized narrative for each piece of art.

Technical Write-Up

AI & Computer Vision:

Custom-trained vision models interpret artwork features. Language models generate thematic descriptions for each piece.

Realtime Experience:

All content is generated live, reflecting the viewer's input and engagement with the installation.

Context

This installation examines how AI mediates meaning, personalizing and transforming cultural narratives for each audience member.
